Investigation Report: Bellarium ($BEL)

Executive Summary

Date: August 21, 2025

Overall Score: 2.3 / 10

Risk Level: EXTREME

Bellarium Network exhibits numerous red flags including anonymous team members, questionable marketing tactics, and widespread scam allegations from community members and independent analysts. The project lacks verifiable development progress and shows characteristics of a potential exit scam.

Proposed Solution
