Investigation Report: Elderglade ($ELDE)

Executive Summary

Date: August 21, 2025

Overall Score: 6.8 / 10

Risk Level: MEDIUM

Recommendation: HOLD

Elderglade represents a legitimate Web3 gaming project with innovative AI-driven mechanics and strong technical foundations. While the project shows significant promise with proper security audits and working mobile game traction, token performance has been poor (-93% from ATH) and team transparency remains limited despite being developed by established studio W3Forge.

1. Project Overview (8 / 10)

Problem Statement

  • Web3 gaming lacks replayability and engaging long-term mechanics
  • Blockchain games often prioritize tokenomics over actual gameplay quality
  • Limited bridge between casual mobile gaming and complex Web3 ecosystems

Proposed Solution

  • AI-driven procedural content generation ensuring unique experiences
  • Hybrid model combining casual mobile match-3 with deep MMORPG mechanics
  • Cross-platform progression allowing mobile achievements to enhance MMORPG gameplay

Assessment: Elderglade addresses real challenges in Web3 gaming with a compelling hybrid approach. The AI integration for procedural content generation is innovative and could solve the replayability issues plaguing many blockchain games. The mobile-to-MMORPG progression system is well-conceived.
