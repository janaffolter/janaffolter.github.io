Investigation Report: Maxi Doge (MAXI)

Executive Summary

Date: August 20, 2025

Overall Score: 4.2 / 10

Risk Level: EXTREME

Recommendation: AVOID

Maxi Doge exhibits multiple characteristics of a high-risk speculative instrument with significant red flags including anonymous leadership, excessive promotional marketing, unsustainable tokenomics, and potential regulatory non-compliance. While smart contract audits provide some legitimacy, the project's fundamental structure and execution raise serious concerns about investor protection.

1. Project Overview (5 / 10)

Problem Statement

  • Claims to address the "lack of alpha opportunities" for retail crypto traders.
  • Targets "degen traders" seeking extreme leverage and high-risk positions.

Proposed Solution

  • Meme coin ecosystem offering staking rewards (up to 2513% APY).
  • Community trading competitions and exclusive "alpha strategies".
  • Planned integrations with futures trading platforms for leveraged positions.

Assessment: No unique technological innovation or real-world utility beyond speculative trading. Value proposition relies entirely on community hype and unsustainable staking rewards that create Ponzi-like mechanics.
