    Professional Identity

    With more than 30 years of experience in technology and transformation, I’ve honed a core capability: reading the present with clarity, identifying structural flaws, and assembling technological, organizational, and human building blocks to create global, durable, and replicable models.

    My journey through big tech, AI, analytics, and distributed architectures has taught me how to design solutions that solve complex, large‑scale problems. Today, I apply this expertise to projects that truly matter—using technology as an amplifier of life, never as a substitute for it.

    As a certified Scrum Product Owner and SAFe Agilist, I embody a deeply rooted Agile mindset: clear vision, collaboration, autonomy, simplicity, and value. I excel at transforming complex ideas into concrete, robust, impact‑driven solutions.

    I believe in an innovation that respects natural systems, optimizes resources, and creates resilient models aligned with today’s economic, social, and ecological realities.

    Inspired by life, for life.
