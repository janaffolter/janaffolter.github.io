    Introduction

    This section highlights the majors projects I have worked on, designed, built, or architected outside my employee roles. These initiatives represent my independent work: self‑driven explorations, sovereign architectures, experimental platforms, and applied innovation efforts developed without corporate constraints.

    They reflect the same principles that guide my professional practice — clarity, systemic thinking, technical rigor, and long‑term architectural coherence — but with the freedom to challenge assumptions, test new patterns, and push emerging technologies into practical form. Each project demonstrates how I approach problem‑solving when I operate autonomously: simplifying complexity, building resilient systems, and turning ideas into working, scalable solutions.

    2026 - TRiE

    TRiE is an identity‑coherence engine I designed to help organizations understand how well a person aligns with a role, a team, or an opportunity. Instead of relying on keywords or HR filtering, TRiE analyzes identity signals, trajectory, and role requirements to produce a clear, objective coherence score.

    TRiE is disruptive because it reframes hiring as an identity‑coherence problem, revealing strong fits, hidden matches, and internal mobility opportunities that traditional ATS systems cannot detect — without exposing any of the underlying technical mechanisms.

    2026 - AUTOFARM™

    AUTOFARM™ is a sovereign agricultural intelligence architecture I designed to create a global, standardized protocol for autonomous farming. Supported by Innosuisse (Project N° 136.046 IMIA‑ENG), the project unifies autonomous local units, causal crop models, and sovereign cognitive cores into a coherent system capable of generating, structuring, and operationalizing agronomic intelligence.

    AUTOFARM™ is disruptive because it reframes agriculture as a cognitive infrastructure — sovereign, resilient, and data‑driven — assembling mature but siloed technologies into a unified protocol. It is built for global institutions, research programs, and mission‑driven partners seeking to modernize agriculture with systemic intelligence rather than incremental tools.

    2025 - Proxy‑KEDA

    Proxy‑KEDA is a Kubernetes‑native scale‑to‑zero orchestration pattern I designed to eliminate idle infrastructure costs while preserving production‑grade performance. It combines KEDA scale‑to‑zero, an intelligent wake‑up proxy, an activation API, and a dynamic application catalog to deliver sub‑5‑second cold starts, 100% idle memory reduction, and zero vendor lock‑in.

    I built Proxy‑KEDA to solve a global, universal problem: organizations waste 30–65% of their Kubernetes budget on idle services. Existing solutions were too complex, too limited, or too cloud‑specific. Proxy‑KEDA provides a sovereign, frugal, production‑ready alternative that brings serverless‑level efficiency to any Kubernetes cluster.

    2025 - JEDI

    JEDI (Just Enough Document Intelligence) is a sovereign document‑intelligence engine I designed for administrations that need to process large volumes of heterogeneous documents. Originating from the phonetic contraction of “GED‑AI”, JEDI combines OCR extraction, semantic understanding, and intelligent classification to transform unstructured documents into searchable, comparable knowledge objects.

    JEDI is disruptive because it gives institutions a sovereign, semantic, real‑time document‑intelligence layer that replaces manual classification, keyword rules, and legacy GED systems — without cloud lock‑in or heavy infrastructure. It unifies fragmented archives, handles real‑world scanned documents, and delivers modernization through a frugal, minimalistic, high‑impact architecture.

    2025 - alphifinance

    alphifinance is a financial‑sovereignty education platform I built to help individuals understand the mindset and systems required for long‑term financial independence. The project teaches compounding, autonomy, and structural financial clarity — without speculation or trading.

    The platform was implemented as a PoC on the Internet Computer Protocol (ICP) to demonstrate how a sovereign cloud can host real educational content without relying on centralized infrastructure. AlphiFinance is disruptive because it reframes financial freedom as a cognitive shift rather than a financial trick, and because it uses sovereign architecture to teach sovereignty itself.

    2025 - xDevOps.ai

    xDevOps.ai is an AI‑augmented DevOps platform that transforms natural‑language intent into deterministic, policy‑validated infrastructure operations. I worked part‑time as Product Owner, helping shape a solution that I considered genuinely disruptive — combining ontology‑driven validation, secure backends, and cognitive troubleshooting to provision and operate cloud and hybrid environments safely.

    I ultimately left the project because, despite its architectural strength, it remained technology‑centric and consistently undersold its value. The focus was on internal mechanisms rather than customer needs, outcomes, and adoption — a direction I could not endorse.
